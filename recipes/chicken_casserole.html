Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

Description

This is one of my Wife's favorite dishes. You can use leftovers but I like to make all the ingredients fresh at time of preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 lb. chicken breast meat or tenders, cubed
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 2 tblsp flour
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups crushed potato chips

Steps

  1. bring water to boil and cook rice for 20 minutes
  2. par cook broccoli for 2-3 minutes
  3. Lightly grease casserole pan
  4. Saute onions in large pan.
  5. Add flour to pan and mix well. Don't let it burn!
  6. Add chicken stock to pan and allow to thicken. Season to taste.
  7. Add cubed chicken to pan and allow to simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Add broccoli to pan and mix well. Remove from heat.
  9. Place cooked rice into casserole pan and spread evenly across the bottom of the pan.
  10. Place chicken and broccoli mixture in casserole pan and spread evenly.
  11. Sprinkle shredded cheese across the top of the mixture in pan.
  12. Sprinkle potato chips across the top and bak in a 350 degree oven for 30-45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
