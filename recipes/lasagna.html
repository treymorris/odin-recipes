Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for one of our family favorites. It uses one unusual ingredient that adds a layer of complexity to the texture and creaminess.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for 10 minutes and cool.
  2. Brown ground beef until cooked through and drain excess grease.
  3. Mix cottage cheese with parmesan.
  4. Grease lasagna pan before assembly.
  5. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Layer sauce, then noodles, then beef, then cottage cheese, provalone, and mozzarella
  7. Repeat until you have made three layers.
  8. Bake for 1 hour
