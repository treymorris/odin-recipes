Pot Roast

Description

This is another family favorite for the fall weather.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast sized according to your needs
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 4 carrots peeled and sliced into large chunks
  • 1 large rutabega peeled and sliced into large chunks
  • 2 cups beef stock

Step

  1. Season chuck roast liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear chuck roast in large braising pan on high heat until both sides are nice and brown. Remove from pan.
  3. Saute onions, rutabega, and carrots on mid high heat for about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add beef stock to pan.
  5. Place chuck roast back in pan with veggies and stock.
  6. Cook in 350 degree oven for 3-3 1/2 hours.
